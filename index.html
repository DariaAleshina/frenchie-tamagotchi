How to Care for Frenchie

  • ⏳ Stats slowly decrease over time – stay attentive!
  • 🍖 Feed to fill the belly & boost energy - available only when really hungry.
  • 🎾 Play to increase happiness (but it tires Frenchie).
  • 🤲 Rub his ears to gently boost happiness.
  • 😴 Let Frenchie Sleep to restore energy. Available when energy drops.
  • 🕒 Buttons need cooldown after each use – choose wisely.
  • 🐶 Keep stats above 0 – otherwise, it's game over!
